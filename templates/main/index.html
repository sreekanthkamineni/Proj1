Our Restaurant

    We serve authentic south india Cuisine which is a popular Andhara, Telanga and Tamilnaduregion. The cuisine is world renowned for it’s spicy tasty food. Over the years, SK Cuisine has mastered the art of using spice to give one’s taste buds the best food experience. The demand for this unique food has led us to take this experience overseas.

    Our 34 seater fine dining Indian restaurant offers a pleasant, inviting atmosphere that is perfect for casual dining with families and friends. The menu features an extensive array of selections like authentic north Indian and south Indian cuisine, Kebab & Curries with our regular impressive lunch special & dinner menu.
